What is CaloriKat?

This app can scan pictures of food and give you an estimate on the calorie amount. It can also give recommendations for what to eat based off of how many calories you have had throughout the day.

How big is the Market?

This app is for everybody that wants to eat healthier and keep track of their daily calories.

What's the status of the app?

This app is currently under alpha testing and is just the first prototype. Howevver, it is currently fully operational and can be downloaded onto Android phones for use.

What makes this app unique?

The ability to scan food in real time and give an approximation of the calories in the food item. Then it takes this data and updates how many more calories you need for the day.

How do we make money?

We expect to mainly generate income from ads but when the app gets enough users we plan to run contract deals with wholesales to allow them to earn higher posisitions on the users screen.

Who are the devlopers?

This app was created by Hack_rs which is a group of Computer Science majors who attend Howard University. The developers are: David Daniels, Bradon Thymes, Alexander Weinberg and Chadille Jones.

What do we want?

We made this app to better the DMV community by trying to help users to better keep track of their calorie intake and give them healthier food options.
